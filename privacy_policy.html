PhysiLearn Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PhysiLearn! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. PhysiLearn is designed to help students learn physics through interactive content, quizzes, and AI-powered assistance.

By using PhysiLearn, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Data: User role (Student or Lecturer), preferences, and settings
  • Authentication Data: Information from Google Sign-In if you choose to use this service

2.2 Educational Data

  • Learning Progress: Course completion status, quiz scores, and topic mastery levels
  • Quiz Results: Answers, time taken, and performance analytics
  • Study Patterns: Time spent on topics, preferred learning methods, and activity logs
  • AI Interactions: Questions asked to our AI Study Buddy and problem-solving requests

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Analytics: App crashes, performance data, and feature usage statistics
  • Network Information: IP address and connection type for service provision

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Educational Services

  • Provide personalized learning experiences and track your progress
  • Generate AI-powered study assistance and problem-solving guidance
  • Create adaptive learning paths based on your performance
  • Enable lecturers to create and manage tests and view student progress

3.2 Account Management

  • Create and maintain your user account
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Send important updates about the app and your account

3.3 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze app usage to improve our services and user experience
  • Debug technical issues and ensure app stability
  • Develop new features based on user needs and behavior

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4.1 Limited Sharing

  • Educational Context: Lecturers can view progress and quiz results of their students within the app
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and analytics services
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

4.2 Data Processing Partners

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database storage, and analytics
  • AI Services: Anonymous queries for study assistance (no personal data shared)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
  • Regular Security Updates: We maintain up-to-date security practices
  • Secure Infrastructure: Using Google Firebase's enterprise-grade security

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after account deletion
  • Learning Progress: Kept to maintain your educational history and progress tracking
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for service improvement
  • Account Deletion: You may request account deletion, after which personal data will be removed within 30 days

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • Access: View and download your personal data through the app settings
  • Correction: Update your profile information and preferences
  • Deletion: Request account deletion through app settings or by contacting us
  • Data Portability: Export your learning progress and quiz history

7.2 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of non-essential notifications in app settings
  • Control email communication preferences
  • Manage data sharing settings within the app

8. Children's Privacy

PhysiLearn is designed for educational use and may be used by students under 13 with parental consent and school supervision:

  • We collect only necessary educational data for students under 13
  • Parental consent is required for children under 13 to use the app
  • We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal information from children
  • Educational institutions using PhysiLearn must ensure COPPA compliance

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place when transferring data internationally, primarily through our use of Google Firebase services which comply with international data protection standards.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending a notification through the app for significant changes

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Third-Party Services

PhysiLearn integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In: Google Privacy Policy

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.
